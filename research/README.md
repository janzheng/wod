### Functional Bulk Program — Dean Turner Deep Dive

Training research from [@DeanTTraining](https://x.com/deanttraining) applied to the functional-bulk program:

- **[barbells-vs-machines.md](barbells-vs-machines.md)** — Why machines are underrated for hypertrophy solo training. Covers chest press, hack squat, back extensions as barbell swaps. Practical for crowded gyms.
- **[rest-periods-between-sets.md](rest-periods-between-sets.md)** — Rest duration effectiveness curve. 2-3 min optimal for hypertrophy. Detailed rest recommendations by exercise type.
- **[s-tier-exercises-dean-turner.md](s-tier-exercises-dean-turner.md)** — Machine-biased S-tier exercise list per muscle group. Maps what's available at Arrilaga vs specialty equipment.
- **[5x-weekly-split-max-gains.md](5x-weekly-split-max-gains.md)** — 5-day split structure (Option A & B). 2x legs/week, push/pull + upper day. Better recovery than 6-day PPL.
- **[progression-readiness.md](progression-readiness.md)** — When to graduate from current split to 5x. Watches for DOMS managability. Revisit at week 4.
