# Incline Cable Overhead Tricep Extension

**Date:** 2026-03-02
**Source:** [@CoachFHM (Francis Melia)](https://x.com/CoachFHM)

## The Exercise

Set up an adjustable bench at ~45° incline facing away from a cable machine. Rope or bar attached to a low/mid pulley behind you. Lie back, arms overhead, and do tricep extensions.

## Why It's Effective

The triceps have three heads — lateral, medial, and **long head**. The long head is the biggest and is uniquely stretched when your arms are overhead. Most people only do pushdowns, which primarily hit the lateral/medial heads in the shortened position.

This exercise is special because:
- **Overhead position** = long head in a deep stretch (where the most growth stimulus happens)
- **Cable resistance** = constant tension through the entire range (unlike DBs which lose tension at the top)
- **Incline bench** = stabilizes your body so you can focus purely on the triceps, no cheating
- **Combo of all three** = arguably the best tricep exercise for hypertrophy

## How It Compares

| Exercise | Long Head | Lateral/Medial | Stability Needed | Equipment |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Cable Pushdown | Low | High | Low | Cable |
| **Incline Cable Overhead Extension** | **High** | Moderate | **Low (bench)** | Cable + Bench |
| DB Overhead Extension | High | Moderate | High (standing) | Dumbbell |
| Skull Crushers | High | High | Moderate | Barbell/EZ-bar + Bench |
| Dips | Moderate | High | High | Dip bars |

## Application to Program

**Week 1:** Used cable tricep pushdown (lateral/medial head focus).

**Week 2+:** Swap in incline cable overhead extension to hit the long head. Ideally do BOTH in the same session:
1. Incline cable overhead extension (stretch position, long head) — 3×10-12
2. Cable pushdown (shortened position, lateral/medial) — 3×10-12

If only time for one, the overhead variation is the better choice for overall tricep development.

**Setup at Arrilaga:** Need cable station + adjustable bench nearby. Position bench facing away from the stack. If the setup doesn't work logistically, fall back to standing cable overhead extension or DB overhead extension.
